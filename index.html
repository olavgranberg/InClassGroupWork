The reference role is about...
The job is to ensure that the needed litteratur, references and quotes are added to the project. Making the answers trustworthy and reliable.

The answer role is about...
A secretary is responsible for taking notes of relevant points from the discussion and writing down the answers for the questions. The note-taker is responsible for adding the tasks and answer them in the available alphabetic bullet points referring to the lectors questions.

The presenter role is about...
Presenter: A presenters role is to present the groups collaborative outcome/result. He can edit the presentation points area but only add short sentences according to bullet points format. No more than 5 points can be added, so they should be precise and well thoughtout

The analyst role is about...
The analysts roles is to note findings and subjects for further discussion during the collaborative open discussions. Furthermore the analysts perspective must be critic and nuanced.
You can only edit the “Question for further Discussion” Element, adding question and thought to be brought up in class.
The analyst must add at least two questions for further discussions.
Each added point will automatically become an question.
