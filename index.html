The reference role is about...
The job is to ensure that the needed litteratur, references and quotes are added to the project. Making the answers trustworthy and reliable.

The answer role is about...
A secretary is responsible for taking notes of relevant points from the discussion and writing down the answers for the questions. The note-take can only answer the available alphabetic/numeric bullet points referring to the lectors questions.

The presenter role is about...
Presenter: A presenters role is to present the groups collaborative outcome/result. He can edit the “presenters textfield” but only add short sentences according to bullet points format.

The analyst role is about...
The analysts roles is to note findings and subjects for further discussion during the collaborative open discussions. Furthermore the analysts perspective must be critic and nuanced.
a) Only one who can edit the “Question for further Discussion” Elements, adding question and thought to be brought up in class.
b) The analyst must add at least two questions to the presenters text field
c) Each Bullet point must have a ‘?’ at the end of the sentence.
